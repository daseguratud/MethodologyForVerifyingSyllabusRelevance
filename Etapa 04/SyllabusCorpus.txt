the technological development of recent years is largely due to the advance in the conception of modern techniques in the design and implementation of electronic circuit among
which highlight digital system as raw material for the majority of electronic devices or components used at home business or academic level this
academic space provides a basic foundation on the analysis of basic digital circuit such as combinational and sequential circuit which are necessary
for the interpretation analysis and development of more complex digital circuit such as cpus controllers and or application specific integratedcircuit asic
additionally digital circuit synthesis techniques are introduced along with design procedures seen from different levels of abstraction which provides a
complete vision to address hardware development problems using various technological options
general objective
develop in the student skills and abilities in the analysis design and implementation of digital system applicable in computing data processing
industrial instrumentation and control
specific objectives
provide the student with the basic concepts of boolean for the analysis and synthesis of combinational circuit
familiarize the student with the concept of memory and its conception at a hardware level for the development of sequential circuit
give the student knowledge about the hardware implementation of finite statemachines

justification
taking into account that the only limitation of the application of the design and synthesis of
digital circuit lies in the imagination of the designer and that most processes
electrical and electronic processes are carried out in digital form both in control and
communications electromedicine image processing etc it is mandatory that the
professional in the area of modern electrical energy learn about modeling techniques and
synthesis of digital circuit with a very high level of integration and speed through the
vhdl hardware description language to be able to compete successfully in the environment
labor
objectives
• introduce the student to the principles of digital logic and the different
digital electronic design techniques
• design simple operational and functional digital circuit with components
discrete and half scale integratedcircuit making use of the principles of
digital logic
• know the different digital architectures for the implementation of circuit
digital such as cpld fpga microcontroller m icrocontroller and
digital signal cpu dsp
• interpret and model digital circuit with a very high level of integration and
speed through the vhdl hardware description language distrital university “franc isco josé de caldas”
