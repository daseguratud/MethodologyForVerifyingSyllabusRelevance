El desarrollo tecnológico de los últimos años se debe en gran medida al avance en la concepción de técnicas modernas en el diseño e implementación de circuitos electrónicos, entre 
los cuales se destacan los sistemas digitales como materia prima de la mayoría de dispositivos o componentes electrónicos usados a nivel domiciliario, empresarial o académico. Este 
espacio académico proporciona una fundamentación básica sobre el análisis de circuitos digitales básicos como son los de tipo combinacional y secuencial, los cuales son necesarios 
para la interpretación, análisis y desarrollo de circuitos digitales de mayor complejidad como procesadores, controladores y/o circuitos integrados de aplicación específica (ASIC). 
Adicionalmente, se introducen técnicas de síntesis de circuitos digitales junto con procedimientos de diseños vistos desde diferentes niveles de abstracción, lo cual proporciona una 
visión completa para abordar problemas de desarrollo hardware usando diversas opciones tecnológicas.
Objetivo General:
Desarrollar en el estudiante habilidades y destrezas en el análisis, diseño e implementación de sistemas digitales aplicables en la computación, el procesamiento de datos, la 
instrumentación y el control industrial. 
Objetivos Específicos: 
1. Proporcionar al estudiante los conceptos básicos de algebra booleana para el análisis y síntesis de circuitos combinacionales.
2. Familiarizar al estudiante con el concepto de memoria y su forma de concepción a nivel hardware para el desarrollo de circuitos secuenciales.
3. Dar al estudiante el conocimiento sobre la forma de implementación en hardware de máquinas de estado finito.
4. Mostrar una visión general de las aplicaciones en las cuales se utilizan tecnologías de microprocesadores, microcontroladores, Dsp´s y FPGA´s.III. JUSTIFICACIÓN DEL ESPACIO ACADÉMICO

The technological development of recent years is largely due to the advance in the conception of modern techniques in the design and implementation of electronic circuits, among 
which highlight digital systems as raw material for the majority of electronic devices or components used at home, business or academic level. This 
academic space provides a basic foundation on the analysis of basic digital circuits such as combinational and sequential circuits, which are necessary 
for the interpretation, analysis and development of more complex digital circuits such as processors, controllers and/or application-specific integrated circuits (ASIC). 
Additionally, digital circuit synthesis techniques are introduced along with design procedures seen from different levels of abstraction, which provides a 
complete vision to address hardware development problems using various technological options.
General Objective:
Develop in the student skills and abilities in the analysis, design and implementation of digital systems applicable in computing, data processing, 
industrial instrumentation and control. 
Specific Objectives: 
1. Provide the student with the basic concepts of Boolean algebra for the analysis and synthesis of combinational circuits.
2. Familiarize the student with the concept of memory and its conception at a hardware level for the development of sequential circuits.
3. Give the student knowledge about the hardware implementation of finite state machines.
4. Show an overview of the applications in which microprocessor, microcontroller, Dsp's and FPGA's technologies are used.III. JUSTIFICATION OF ACADEMIC SPACE

2. Justificación 
Teniendo en  cuenta que la  única limit ación de  la  aplicación del  diseño  y  síntesis de 
circuitos digitales radica en la imaginación del diseñador, y que la mayoría  de los procesos 
eléctricos y electrónicos se llevan a cabo en forma digital tanto en control como 
comunicaciones, electromedicina, procesamiento de imágenes etc., es obligatorio que el 
profesional en el área de la energía eléctrica moderno conozca  las técnicas de modelado y 
síntesis  de circuitos digitales de muy alto nivel de integración y velocidad, a través del 
lenguaje de descripción de hardware VHDL, para pod er competir  con éxito en el ambiente 
laboral. 
 
3. Objetivos 
• Introducir al estudiante en los principios de la lógica digital, y en las diferentes 
técnicas de diseño electrónico digital. 
• Diseñar  circuitos  digitales  simples,  operativos  y  funcionales  con  componentes 
discretos y circuitos integrados de media escala, haciendo uso de los principios de la 
lógica digital. 
• Conocer las diferentes arquitecturas digitales para la implementación de circuitos 
digitales, tales como: CPLD, FPGA, Microprocesador, M icrocontrolador y 
Procesador Digital de Señales (DSP). 
• Interpretar  y  modelar  circuitos  digitales  de  muy  alto  nivel  de  integración  y 
velocidad, a través del lenguaje de descripción de h ardware VHDL.UNIVERSIDAD  DISTRITAL “FRANC ISCO  JOSÉ DE CALDAS”

2. Justification 
Taking into account that the only limitation of the application of the design and synthesis of 
digital circuits lies in the imagination of the designer, and that most processes 
Electrical and electronic processes are carried out in digital form, both in control and 
communications, electromedicine, image processing, etc., it is mandatory that the 
professional in the area of modern electrical energy learn about modeling techniques and 
synthesis of digital circuits with a very high level of integration and speed, through the 
VHDL hardware description language, to be able to compete successfully in the environment 
labor. 
 
3. Objectives 
• Introduce the student to the principles of digital logic, and the different 
digital electronic design techniques. 
• Design simple, operational and functional digital circuits with components 
discrete and half-scale integrated circuits, making use of the principles of 
digital logic. 
• Know the different digital architectures for the implementation of circuits 
digital, such as: CPLD, FPGA, Microprocessor, M icrocontroller and 
Digital Signal Processor (DSP). 
• Interpret and model digital circuits with a very high level of integration and 
speed, through the VHDL hardware description language. DISTRITAL UNIVERSITY “FRANC ISCO JOSÉ DE CALDAS” 
Technological Faculty 
Technology in Medium and Low Voltage Electrical Systems 
articulated by propaedeutic cycles with Electrical Engineering 2016 -I

• Design, analyze, model, simulate, synthesize and program digital circuits with 
help of CAD-EDA tools based on digital technologies such as CPLD 
and FPGA. 
 
4. Requirements 
As a pre-knowledge, this course requires basic knowledge of devices 
semiconductors and DC circuit theory. It is desirable to use a language of 
programming, but it is not an essential requirement. For the development of the course, it is required 
access to a personal computer with the appropriate software, and access to a laboratory to 
Basic circuit practices. 
 
5. Pedagogical aspects 
The proposal developed by the group of teachers of the Technology curricular project 
in Electricity and Electrical Engineering, started from the analysis of the general characteristics that 
Every technologist must have, as a professional in the electrical sector, in addition to the 
specific knowledge specific to the application of your career that everyone must possess 
engineer, and are detailed in the professional profile that is part of the proposal 
for the transition to academic credits. 
 
Such characteristics, fused within the academic spaces of the curriculum 
they are: 
 
• High level of development of their communication skills. 
• Skills in defining problems, collecting and evaluating information, and developing 
real and efficient solutions. 
• Teamwork capabilities, ability to work with others. 
• Ability to use all of the above to address problems in the complex 
real world. 
 
All academic spaces in the curriculum, like this one, are considered 
theoretical-practical, supporting this dynamic in a problem or question that the student
